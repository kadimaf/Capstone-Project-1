Who We Are

A Community Built on Purpose

The Congolese Association for Congo Development (CACODEV) is a community-focused organization dedicated to creating opportunities for growth, development, and social change.

Founded on the principles of integrity, inclusiveness, and impact, we bring together Congolese citizens and friends of the Congo to promote development, preserve cultural heritage, and foster mutual support.

Through education, advocacy, and community service, we empower individuals to be agents of change in their neighborhoods and beyond.
